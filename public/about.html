ABOUT GYMGUIDE

The problem, the solution, and how it all works.

🎯

The Problem

Gym-goers waste time and feel overwhelmed because fitness information is scattered across YouTube, paywalled apps, and complex forums. When equipment is taken or a routine is forgotten, workouts fall apart. Beginners don't know which exercises target which muscles. Nobody has time for that friction.

💡

The Solution

GymGuide is a streamlined workout companion that gives you instant exercise recommendations filtered by muscle group and equipment. No ads. No paywalls. No login required. Your routines are saved to the cloud and persist across sessions. Just the information you need to work out effectively in seconds.

  • ✓ Browse 1,300+ exercises by muscle group
  • ✓ Filter by available equipment
  • ✓ View animated GIFs and step-by-step form instructions
  • ✓ Save custom routines for consistent weekly training
  • ✓ Log completed workouts and track your week
👥

Who This Is For

  • Gym beginners learning proper exercise selection
  • College students navigating crowded campus gyms
  • Home workout enthusiasts with limited equipment
  • Busy professionals needing efficient, repeatable routines
📊

Data Sources

ExerciseDB API (via RapidAPI) — 1,300+ exercises with animated GIFs, categorized by muscle group and equipment, with professional form instructions.

Supabase (PostgreSQL) — Cloud database storing your saved routines and workout logs.

⚠️

Limitations

  • Exercise GIFs require an internet connection
  • Some niche exercises may not be in the database
  • No user accounts yet — data tied to your browser session ID
  • ExerciseDB free tier limits API requests per day
🛠️
